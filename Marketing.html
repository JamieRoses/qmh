Our Special Mango Toppings

Tapioca

A tapioca pearl is a translucent sphere produced from tapioca, a starch made from the cassava root. A delightful chewy topping that pairs perfectly with your mango drink.

Nata De Coco

Nata de coco is a unique Filipino specialty that is made with fermented coconut water. A sweet, chewy coconut jelly pieces that add texture to your drink.

Crushed Oreo

The original Oreo cookie crushed in small pieces cream-free, ready to use for your recipes. A crunchy and sweet twist that complements mango perfectly.

Crushed Graham

Graham cracker crumbs are made by crushing graham crackers, a type of flat and crisp cookie made with graham flour and sweetened with honey. A delightful blend of graham crumbs for added flavor and crunch.

Marshmallows

Marshmallows are made of sugar, corn syrup, water and gelatin. Soft, fluffy marshmallows that melt perfectly in your drink.
