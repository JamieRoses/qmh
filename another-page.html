About

Mango Tapioca is a refreshing tropical dessert, With yummy mango chunks, and tapioca pearls are mixed with milk based cream. This is unique because it combines the chewy texture of tapioca pearls with the vibrant, tropical flavor of mango, creating a refreshing, creamy dessert with a contrasting play between the soft tapioca and the juicy fruit, often served with coconut milk for added richness, making it a popular choice in Asian cuisine, particularly during hot weather.
